Problem to solve

Write a JavaScript program that computes first 100 Fibonacci numbers.

The first two Fibonacci numbers are 1 and 1.

To calculate the Nth Fibonacci number add the (N-1)th and (N-2)th numbers together.

The first few are therefore 1, 1, 1+1=2, 1+2=3, 2+3=5, 3+5=8
